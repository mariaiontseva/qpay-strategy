035 Features · 36 Months · Staged Roadmap

What QPay ships, and in what order.

Thirty-five features across three phases. MVP proves the wedge for the first 500-2,000 UK Ltds. V2 closes adjacent gaps that force customers onto second tools. V3 thickens the platform into a full business operating layer.

MVP 16 features · months 0-12
Prove the wedge
EMI account, director/shareholder flow, accountant-grade books, compliance calendar. Delivered through Q channel first.
V2 10 features · months 13-24
Close adjacent gaps
Payroll, CT600, FX, expense cards, resolution library. Reasons customers stop leaving for second tools.
V3 9 features · months 25-36
Platform thickening
Insurance, lending, API, automated filings, advisory. From "software that moves money well" to "the Ltd OS".
