The gap

SMEs are stuck in the missing middle.

Mainstream banks offer rigid, general-purpose accounts. Consumer fintechs prioritise card spend, budgeting, and sole traders. Neither is built for an incorporated SME running payroll, paying suppliers and approving payments across a small team.

  • Mainstream banks — limited automation, slow workflows
  • Consumer fintechs — built for sole traders, not teams
  • Multi-currency platforms — FX-led, irrelevant for UK-domestic SMEs
  • Card-led tools — spend-tracking, not payment operations
  • Accounting software — handles books, not payments
The platform

A purpose-built payments platform.

QPayments handles the day-to-day financial operations of a UK Ltd — Faster Payments, scheduled payroll, supplier batches and multi-user controls — without cards, lending or FX overhead.

01 · Pay Real-time Faster Payments Send and receive in GBP via your UK sort code and account number. Domestic, instant, EMI-aligned.
02 · Schedule Payroll & supplier batches Schedule outgoing payments and upload batch files for payroll runs and recurring supplier settlements.
03 · Control Multi-user roles Preparer, approver and viewer permissions for finance teams that need internal approval flows.
04 · Report Tax-ready exports Categorised transaction data, exportable in formats your accountant or bookkeeping software can read.
01Card-free

No cards, by design.

QPayments deliberately excludes physical and virtual debit cards. The result is lower fraud exposure, simpler operations, and a focused payments-first product for businesses that don't need card spend.

02UK domestic

Built for the UK Ltd.

Account-to-account payments in GBP via Faster Payments. No FX, no multi-currency complexity. Made for UK-domestic SMEs with structured payroll, supplier and recurring obligations.

Strong customer authentication, role-based access, encrypted communications and daily reconciliations — built around the obligations of an Electronic Money Institution from day one.
