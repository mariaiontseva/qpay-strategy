01Card-free

No cards, by design.

QPayments deliberately excludes physical and virtual debit cards. The result is lower fraud exposure, simpler operations, and a focused payments-first product for businesses that don't need card spend.

02UK domestic

Built for the UK Ltd.

Account-to-account payments in GBP via Faster Payments. No FX, no multi-currency complexity. Made for UK-domestic SMEs with structured payroll, supplier and recurring obligations.

03Compliance-led

Architected for EMI rules.

Strong customer authentication, role-based access, encrypted communications and daily reconciliations — built around the obligations of an Electronic Money Institution from day one.
